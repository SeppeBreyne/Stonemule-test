The Band

Founded in 2014 by Tom Vandaele (Urge for blood, GOD), Benjamin Himpens (Grounded), Ruben Dewilde (Urge for Blood) en Dries Herreman (GOD) this band initially started off as a hardcore/punk band. The band mainly played local shows in the west of Flanders. In 2015 bassist Dries decided to part ways with the band to persue his dream of travelling the world.

Wannes Breyne (Hadron highway, Always fallen) filled in his spot, opening the possibility to add another vocal in future musical endeavors. In 2016 the band decided to go on a break to focus on writing new songs. The desire to no longer restrict themselves to a specific genre, and the will to combine different styles of heavy music became the main thought during this creative proces.

What they say about us

“You can best describe these songs as kyuss coated with sludge, a much needed work attitude and a portion of guts put in front of us on stage. As stubborn mules that do what they want they sew pitchblack riffs with great precision. To paint a pitchblack still with the electric guitar, is a skill possessed by few guitarists. The alternating vocals of front vocalists Tom Vandaele and Benjamin Himpens even created some Mastodon magic.” (Translated from Dutch)
Between 2016 – 2017 2 singles where released, and finally in 2018 their first EP “Dystopian State” became a fact.
